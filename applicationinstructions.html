Application Instructions

If you are interested in joining the Undergraduate Journal of Psychology at Berkeley, please send an email to editorial AT ujpb.org with the following information:

  • Name
  • Year
  • Major or Intended Major
  • A current resume
  • A writing sample (editors only)
  • A portfolio sample (graphic designers only)

We accept applications for graphic designers and website managers on a rolling basis. Editor applications are accepted and evaluated at the beginning of the fall and spring semesters.
